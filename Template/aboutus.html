About PedalShare

We're on a mission to make campus commuting smarter, greener, and more affordable — one pedal at a time.

Our Mission

PedalShare was born out of a simple idea — students shouldn't have to walk long distances across campus or spend money on auto-rickshaws when there are perfectly good cycles sitting idle in hostels.

We connect cycle owners with riders in a seamless, wallet-based marketplace. Owners earn passive income, riders save money, and the planet breathes a little easier.

Every ride on PedalShare is a step toward a more sustainable, connected campus community.

What We Stand For

The principles that guide every decision we make.

Sustainability

Zero emissions, zero fuel, zero guilt. Cycling is the greenest way to get around.

Affordability

Rides starting at ₹15/hr. We believe sustainable transport should be accessible to every student.

Trust & Safety

Every owner is a verified student. Your wallet is secured and every transaction is logged.

Simplicity

Book a cycle in under 60 seconds. No paperwork, no deposits, no friction.
