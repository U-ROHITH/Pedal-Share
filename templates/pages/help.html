Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click Sign Up in the top navbar. Enter your full name, email, and a password (minimum 8 characters). You'll instantly receive ₹500 free in your wallet — no credit card needed. You're then logged in and ready to book.

How do I book a cycle?

Go to Available Cycles. Find a cycle you like, enter the number of hours you need, and click Book Now. The total cost is deducted from your wallet instantly and your booking appears under My Bookings.

Can I cancel a booking? Will I get a refund?

Yes! Go to My Bookings and click Cancel on any active booking. You get a 100% full refund instantly back to your PedalShare wallet.

How do I mark a ride as completed?

Once you've returned the cycle to the owner, go to My Bookings and click Complete Ride. This marks the booking as done and makes the cycle available for other students again.

How do I add money to my wallet?

Go to Wallet in the navbar. Enter the amount (₹1 – ₹10,000) and click Add to Wallet. Your balance updates immediately and a transaction record is created.

How do I list my own cycle for renting?

Click My Cycles in the navbar, then List a Cycle. Fill in the title, type (regular/hybrid/mountain/electric), colour, price per hour, pickup location, and availability times. Your cycle will appear in the marketplace instantly.

How do I remove my cycle from the marketplace?

Go to My Cycles and click the trash icon on the cycle you want to remove. If someone has an active booking on it, they'll automatically receive a full refund before the listing is deleted.

I forgot my password / can't sign in

Make sure you're using the email address you registered with. Passwords are case-sensitive. If you're still stuck, contact our support team and we'll help you regain access.

Is my wallet and payment information safe?

Yes. All transactions are logged and protected. Your wallet balance is never stored as raw currency outside our secured database. Payment gateway connections use 256-bit SSL encryption.

How do I report a problem or raise a complaint?

Use the Raise a Complaint page. Fill in the category, your booking reference (if applicable), and a description. We respond within 48 hours.
